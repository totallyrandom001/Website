Server Rules

Joining

  1. You must read all guideline channels and choose your department + subdepartment
  2. You must post your timezone and when you get online + when you get offline in the timezones channel

Activity

  1. You must be active in our server. We define activity as: Joining at least 1 deployment per 3 days and sometimes talking in chat.
  2. If you are unable to, please state so using the guidelines in the inactivity notice channel.

Deployments

  1. Remember to clock in when you join a deployment, and clock out after you leave.
  2. Comply with higher ups' instructions.
  3. Keep a watch on the chat for anything important.
