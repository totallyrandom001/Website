Quickstart

  • Check all buttons: Make sure silent alarms are off and the term door is closed
  • Reset keypad

Policies

  • At least 1 CR personnel must be inside the control room at all times
  • Shifts must be fixed to 20 minutes

Reminder

If you hear a buzzer, it’s from the term room door. Check who it is with the old monitor (camera 2) and allow them to pass.

Visual screening guide

  • Tell person to stand on the red circle
  • Tell person to spin slowly with their hands up

Remember

  • If someone has contraband, tell them to double check that they dropped all guns. If they dont drop the gun, terminate them and send their username in the blacklists channel.

Blacklists

All people who enter must be blacklist checked. Check here.

  • Nothing written – Don't let in. Use lethal force if necessary.
  • SB – Don’t use lethal force unless necessary
  • Exclamation – Use lethal force, kill no matter the cost
