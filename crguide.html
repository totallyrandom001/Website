Control Room Guide

Laptop: Facility CCTV

Old monitor: Airlock & Term room exit CCTV

Blue button: Inner door

Brown button: Term door unlock (Leave pressed)

Right buttons: Cell doors unlock

Bottom right: All doors lock, Emergency lockdown

Top right: Reset emergency lockdown

Yellow button: Medical bay

Green button: Food room

Orange button: Locker room

Door button: Opens all cell doors

Card button: Shows cards status

Green light: Card active

Red light: Card inactive

Keycard reader on monitor: Use keycard to open airlock door

Blue locked door: Term hallway door, can only be opened with card

Camera system: Use to monitor activity throughout the facility

Control room operator duties: Monitor CCTV, control doors, manage lockdowns

Note: Always communicate with security when initiating lockdowns
